Our Story

Huntington's Hometown Diner

The Country Post is the kind of place that reminds you why small towns feel like home. Tucked right here in Huntington County, we've been serving hearty, home-cooked meals to families, farmers, and regulars since the beginning — and we're not planning on changing anytime soon.

From our famous broasted chicken and hand-cut tenderloins to all-day breakfast and freshly baked pie, every plate is made from scratch with genuine care. Walk in a stranger, leave feeling like a regular.
